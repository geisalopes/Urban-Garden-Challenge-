Why Having Urban Gardens?

Urban gardens are precious green spaces amidst the hustle and bustle of cities. They offer a number of benefits for the environment, as well as for the physical and mental well-being of people living in urban areas. This website is dedicated to exploring the importance of urban gardens and providing resources for those interested in taking part in this cause.

Benefits of Urban Gardens

  • Reducing urban heat
  • Improving air quality
  • Promotion of biodiversity
  • Mental and physical well-being
